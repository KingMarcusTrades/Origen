The problem

Sourcing exceptional
green coffee is
harder than it should be.

01
You can't verify quality before committing
Buying a full lot based on a spec sheet is a gamble. One bad harvest and your signature blend is off for a season.
02
The supply chain is opaque and slow
Brokers, intermediaries, and importers all add margin and remove accountability. By the time beans reach you, the story is gone.
03
Smaller roasters get the worst terms
Volume buyers get priority access and better pricing. As an independent you're last in line — and first to be told a lot sold out.
